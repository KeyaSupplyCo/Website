About Keya Supply Co

Founded in 2026, we specialize in bulk wholesale PET and PP strapping — sourced directly from ISO-certified manufacturers, delivered across North America without the middlemen.

Heavy-Duty PET Straps

High-tensile polyester strapping with minimal stretch. Ideal for steel coils, lumber, construction materials, and industrial equipment. Weather-resistant for outdoor use.

AAR Approved Straps

Association of American Railroads certified strapping for rail freight. Smooth and embossed types available — meeting all AAR specs for safe, compliant transit.

Polypropylene Straps

Lightweight, economical solution for light-to-medium loads. Excellent flexibility for carton bundling, newspaper packaging, and palletizing. Hand & machine grade available.

Custom Specifications

Need a specific width, thickness, or color? We work directly with manufacturers to fulfill custom orders. Volume pricing available for pallets and truckloads.

EDI Compatible

Streamlined order placement and demand forecasting integration. We make procurement frictionless — connect your system and let supply take care of itself.

Reliable Supply Chain

Maintained inventory and streamlined cross-border logistics from Windsor, ON to Detroit, MI and all of North America. Fast, hassle-free delivery — pallet or truckload.

Eco-Friendly PET

Our PET strapping is fully recyclable, contributing to a greener supply chain. Environmentally responsible packaging that meets modern sustainability goals.

Bulk Discounts

Direct manufacturer sourcing means we cut out the middlemen and pass the savings to you. Volume-tiered pricing from single pallets up to full truckloads.
